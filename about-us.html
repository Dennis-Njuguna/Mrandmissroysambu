Mr. & Miss Roysambu 2025 was more than just a pageant — it was a vibrant celebration of youth, talent, and the power of mental health awareness in our community.

This groundbreaking event was created and hosted by WUEEH TV, a youth-driven platform committed to amplifying young voices, nurturing creative expression, and promoting social change at the grassroots level. With a passionate belief in the potential of our youth,WUEEH TV brought together talented individuals from Roysambu Sub-County and beyond, uniting them under one powerful theme. WUEEH TV is a CBC company committed to bridging the gap between main stream media and the community, as well as empowering the youth.

From the captivating beauty pageant and energetic dance performances to mental health talks, acrobatic shows, and live music, the event served as a safe and expressive space for young people to shine, speak, and support each other.

Our Vision:

To inspire mental health resilience and awareness through the creative power of community-based events that empower, educate, and uplift.

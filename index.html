Promoting Mental Health Resilience through Talent and Creativity

At WUEEH TV, we believe in the power of community, creativity, and conversation to spark real change. As part of our ongoing commitment to mental health advocacy, we proudly organized the MR & MISS ROYSAMBU 2025 event — an initiative aimed at promoting mental health resilience among youth through the transformative power of talent and artistic expression.

This year’s event brought together passionate and talented youth from Roysambu Sub-County and beyond, creating a safe and vibrant platform where individuals could not only showcase their gifts, but also engage in open discussions around mental health, stress management, peer support, and emotional well-being.

WUEEH TV is a youth-driven media platform dedicated to inspiring positive change through storytelling, creativity, and community engagement. Our mission is to amplify youth voices, promote mental health awareness, and build resilient communities across Kenya and beyond. Through events, digital content, and grassroots initiatives, we create safe spaces where young people can express themselves, connect, and grow. We are a CBC company committed to bridging the gap between main stream media and the community, as well as empowering the youth.
